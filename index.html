audrey hepburn

Beautiful and unique

Kathleen Ruston, better known as Audrey Hepburn, was born on May 04, 1929 in Belgium. Endowed with a great talent for languages ​​and literary culture, she knew how to forge a personality that stood out from the other actors of this time.
Taking ballerina lessons in her youth in the hopes of becoming a principal dancer and working occasionally as a model, it was ultimately in the cinematographic and theatrical world that she made her career. She is one of those actresses who made Hollywood cinema shine around the world and whose works will forever remain timeless.
It was in January 1993, that we left the magnificent Audrey Hepburn, then 63 years old.
